As a clinical psychologist I offer counseling for people in all walks of life.

I provide individual and group counseling, both in-person and virtually. My approach is solution-focused and tailored to the individual needs of my clients.

<img src="{{ '/images/illustrations/therapy.png' | relative_url }}" alt="Therapy illustration" style="float: right; width: 380px; margin: 0 0 1.5rem 2rem;" />

## Topics I Counsel On

* Professional challenges
  * Workplace conflicts
  * Burnout
  * Work-life balance
  * Developing stress management strategies
  * Navigating challenges for women in the workplace

* Career reorientation and re-entry
  * Lack of motivation
  * Missing perspective
  * Challenges along the educational path

* Life crises
  * Separation, divorce, loss of a loved one
  * Bullying
  * Family conflicts
  * Challenges in dealing with children

* Mental health conditions
  * Stressful situations
  * Anxiety and panic
  * Depression

* Trauma
  * Traumatic experiences
  * Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)

* Illness
  * How can I cope with a (chronic) illness?
  * Experience with conditions such as multiple sclerosis, cancer, Parkinson's, arthritis, diabetes.
