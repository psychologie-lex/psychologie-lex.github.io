I offer clinical-psychological counseling and treatment for adults and adolescents aged 17 and above.

## Confidentiality

As a clinical psychologist, I am bound by statutory confidentiality obligations under the Psychology Act 2013 (§ 37). All session content is treated with strict confidentiality.

## Initial Session

In the initial session we clarify your concerns, your expectations, and how we might proceed. Together we decide whether counseling or clinical-psychological treatment is the most appropriate course.

## Duration and Frequency

Each session is 60 minutes: 50 minutes of conversation and 10 minutes for preparation, follow-up, documentation, and administrative tasks.

The frequency and number of sessions (e.g. weekly or biweekly) are agreed upon together.

- **Treatment/Therapy:** typically 10–30 sessions; longer-term support is possible if needed.
- **Counseling:** tailored to your individual needs and often shorter in duration.


## Fees and Payment

- New clients receive their first session at half price.
- Payment by bank transfer (due within 14 days) or cash after the session.
- Cancellations with less than 48 hours' notice will be charged in full.

All services are exempt from VAT pursuant to § 6 para. 1 no. 19 (medical treatment) and § 6 para. 1 no. 27 (small-business regulation) of the Austrian VAT Act.

## Health Insurance Coverage

Since January 2024, **clinical-psychological treatment** in Austria is a statutory health insurance benefit, on a par with medical care.

This means that costs can — depending on availability — be **fully covered by health insurance** (a Kassenplatz/contracted slot), or you may be entitled to a **partial reimbursement** for privately paid sessions.

### Partial Reimbursement Without a Contracted Slot

Even without an assigned contracted slot, you can access clinical-psychological treatment privately and apply for **partial cost reimbursement** from your statutory health insurer (ÖGK, SVS, or BVAEB). In this case, you pay the fee upfront and then submit a claim for reimbursement. The amount varies by insurer and typically covers only a portion of the costs. Requirements may include a medical confirmation and prior approval from the insurer. Some **private health insurance** plans also cover the costs fully or partially, depending on your policy.

The specific rules (e.g. application process, approval, reimbursement amount) differ by insurer (ÖGK, SVS, BVAEB).

### Access to Fully Funded Contracted Slots

Contracted slots are currently assigned **not directly through the practice** but exclusively via the central platform Psyhelp. Private appointments can be booked directly with me.

Fully funded contracted slots are allocated centrally through **Psyhelp**: [https://www.psyhelp.at](https://www.psyhelp.at). The process works as follows:


1. **Registration:**
   Register online and provide information about your concerns and preferences (e.g. location, setting).

2. **Clearing:**
   A professional initial assessment helps determine the level and urgency of support needed.

3. **Referral:**
   You will be matched with a suitable clinical psychologist near you as soon as a contracted slot becomes available.

4. **Contact:**
   You will receive all relevant information by email and can arrange an initial appointment directly.
