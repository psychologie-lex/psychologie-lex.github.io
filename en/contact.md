# Practice

For in-person appointments you will find me at the accessible shared practice [Fleissner, Logopädie & Co](https://www.fleissner-logopaedie.at/) at [Körösistraße 64a, 8010 Graz, Austria]({{ site.data.contact.maps_link }}).

The practice is located in the same building as the Bäckerei Auer in the southern part of the building.

We are easily accessible by public transport (stop Hasnerplatz for tram lines 3 and 5), and there is parking available (blue zone), as well as a parking garage [Geidorf Center](https://maps.app.goo.gl/nbGxSA6qYUnCnvAH6).

# Virtual Appointments

For virtual appointments I use [Zoom](https://zoom.us/). After scheduling your appointment you will receive a link to the virtual meeting room which you can simply click to join. Please ensure you have a stable internet connection and a device with a camera and microphone.
